Title : Accident in Paris.

- Carac = Caracteristics ; 
- Com = Town (3-digit number, consisting of 1 [for Paris] followed by the number of the district);
- Carr = crossroad ; (RGU code : Unique Geographic Référentiel)
- Segm = track segment ; 
- NomV = street name ; 
- Vehi1 = first vehicle involved; 
- CAdmin = administrative category of vehicle, 
- LVeh = letter of the vehicle involved (the vehi1 the letter A, the vehi2 the letter B, the vehi3, if there is a third, the letter C, the letter Z means "Fleeing vehicle" ;
- CatU = user category (BL = injured lighter, BH = injured hospitalized)

Source : Direction de la Voirie et des déplacements - Agence de la mobilité
Time Period : 01 January 12 - 31 December 13
